**LORE — Yuko & Dante, opening scene**

Yuko is building the shop. It is not finished. This is how he finds her.

Dante has just returned from delivering the girl to the dimension where her parents are alive. He has updates. Everyone is happy. By tomorrow they will not remember it was ever different. This is the nature of the work.

Dante starts moving furniture without being asked. Yuko does not tell him where anything goes. She is not withholding — she is watching him figure out where things want to be. He doesn't owe her for the girl. She doesn't owe him direction. The furniture is outside the transaction entirely.

Yuko already knew he would help. She premembers. This is not payment and she will not let it become one.

The heavy cabinet is the last thing. He asks where it goes.

She tells him where she'll put it herself.

Then: *"You didn't think helping me move in was payment, did you?"*

This is the first moment he understands what kind of woman she is.

He still doesn't know she'd have done it free.
